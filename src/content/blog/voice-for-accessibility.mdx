For a lot of people, the written word is a wall. Not because they aren't intelligent, not because they don't care — but because the way their brain processes text makes reading exhausting, slow, or impossible. Dyslexia, ALS, low vision, and low literacy affect hundreds of millions of people worldwide. The tools built to help them have historically been clunky, expensive, or stigmatizing. That's starting to change.

## [ElevenReader: A Reading App That Actually Sounds Human](https://try.elevenlabs.io/8jq9xhb1mno4)

ElevenLabs built [ElevenReader](https://try.elevenlabs.io/8jq9xhb1mno4) as a free app that converts text — articles, PDFs, ebooks, web pages — into spoken audio using its AI voice technology. The difference between this and a standard screen reader is immediately obvious the first time you hear it. There are no robotic cadences, no mispronounced words every third sentence. It sounds like a person reading to you.

For someone with dyslexia, that matters more than most people realize. Struggling readers often know the content they want to consume — they just can't get to it without friction. Being able to paste in any article or document and hear it read back clearly removes that barrier without requiring any special accommodations at work or school. You just use your phone like everyone else.

For people with low literacy or who are learning a new language, the same tool works as an on-demand tutor. Hear the words. Follow along. Understand without having to decode every character.

## [Voice Cloning for People Who Are Losing Their Voice](https://try.elevenlabs.io/8jq9xhb1mno4)

ALS is a progressive disease that eventually takes away a person's ability to speak. What ElevenLabs offers here is something genuinely significant: the ability to clone your own voice before that happens.

The process works by recording a sample of your voice — a few minutes of clear audio is enough to start, though more recordings improve quality. ElevenLabs processes that audio and creates a synthetic version of your voice that can speak any text you type into it. When speaking becomes difficult or impossible, you still sound like yourself. Not a generic text-to-speech robot. You.

This use case has made real differences in people's lives. The ability to keep communicating in your own voice — to leave voice messages, to talk to your kids, to run a meeting — carries emotional weight that a generic voice assistant simply cannot.

## [Setting It Up Takes Minutes](https://try.elevenlabs.io/8jq9xhb1mno4)

For ElevenReader, download the app, create a free account, and start importing content. You can choose from a library of available voices or use a cloned voice you've already created. Adjusting reading speed is straightforward. The app supports a wide range of file formats.

For voice cloning, log into ElevenLabs, navigate to the Voice Lab, and select Instant Voice Clone. Record or upload your audio, name the voice, and save it. From that point forward, you can use it anywhere in the platform — including piping it into ElevenReader.

Neither of these setups requires technical knowledge. If you can use a smartphone and a basic web app, you can do this.
